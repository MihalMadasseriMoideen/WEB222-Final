Football

Football is one of the most popular sport played around the world. It's played by two teams in which they try to score goal by hitting the ball to opposite teams net. Soccer is an another name for football. There are many football competitions conducted throughout the year. It is also a thrilling game as in the game each team member tries to score higher number of goals in the provided time. The modernized version of football is formed in england during 19th century. Today it is governed by FIFA. They used to conduct many global competitions which encourages all the football players throughout the world.

Famous Players

There are many great football players in the world. In that some of them are Lionel Messi, Ronaldo, Neymar,etc.
