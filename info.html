History of Football

Football is one of the gameplay which is known all over the world. In 19th century the modern football was introduced at Britain. Before that it's used to be played by people in towns and villages. During that time there was custom rules created by the playing people and the rules changes according to the places and people. Later some schools and residence teams taken that as winter game. During that time also the situation of rules was same. Each school had different rules. Later for conducting tournaments the University of Cambridge made a rule for all students. Later in 1863 there was many meetings and finally they accomplished the rules for football, in that they removed the carrying of ball by hand. They decided to allow only goalkeepers to use their hands to carry ball during the gameplay. Later started to undertake competitions between schools, residence, places, etc and finally it become the popular game and now there are matches played between countries.

Rules of Football

Lets checkout some of the rules of professional football.

  • The timing of the play is total 90 minutes and there will be an extra 15 minute break after 45 minutes.
  • There will be 11 players including goalkeeper.
  • The field should be natural or artificial grass.
  • The ball must be circular and it should have 58-61 cm circumference
  • Each team can have substitute players.
  • There must be a referee and two assistant referee's.
  • If the score is tie after 90 minutes, extra time will be allocated.
  • If the score is still equal after extra time, penalty will take place.
  • Foul play will be noted by referee and may receive yellow or red card

How to play Football

Lets checkout the steps for playing professional football.

  • Understand the rules of the game.
  • Know the duration of the match.
  • Know the status of the game.
  • Perform the task like defending or forward.
  • Be aware of the score.
  • Pass the ball to other team members for scoring goals.
  • Try to mark opposite team members and thereby track the ball.
  • Try to practice more before the match.

Tips for winning

Lets checkout few tips for winning football.

  • Always be confident and play hard.
  • Be aware about the match status.
  • Always mark opposite team players as they may suddenly strike to your post..
  • Pass the ball forward as you get chance.
  • The costume should be perfect.
  • Always concentrate on match.
